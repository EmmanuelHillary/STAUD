About us

WHO WE ARE

Staud is the leading real estate property center platform in Nigeria. With a web-based platform for property rentals and sales, we provide users with the best property search experience both online and offline by connecting them with legitimate and verified real estate agents. In the Nigerian property market, with over 60,000 property listings which include properties like homes, houses, lands, shops, office spaces and other commercial properties, small, medium and large-scale real estate companies in Nigeria who desire to scale up the sales and rentals of their properties can partner with Staud. It is founded by Kaduru George and Adebowale Paul.

OUR VISION

Here at Staud, we hope to make finding accommodation for students super easy and stress free. We hope to make our clients find a place they can call home at the best and most affordable prices. We hope to be the nation’s leading real estate online agency. We hope to be the best in what we do.

OUR MISSION

We are the nation’s leading real estate firm, we deal with all kinds of student accommodations, right from on campus to off campuses. Our mission with this is be the number 1 stop point for every university student anywhere in the country when it comes to finding accomodation
